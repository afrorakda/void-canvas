# #100 Void Canvas

**Void Canvas** is the final tool in the Fridge Combo series. It is a minimalist digital detox space designed for raw, unfiltered output. Write your thoughts, release them, and let them vanish into the void. No logs, no history, just pure mental clarity.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action
- **One Action**: Releases your temporary thoughts into nothingness with a "fog-out" animation.

## Features
- **Ephemeral Thinking**: Nothing is saved. Your words exist only in the moment.
- **Fog Animation**: Experience a visual sense of relief as your text fades away like mist.
- **Focus-First UI**: A massive, distraction-free canvas optimized for deep thought.
- **Privacy Guaranteed**: No login, no tracking, and no database. What happens on the canvas, stays on the canvas—until it doesn't.

## How to Use
1. **Pour**: Write down anything—stress, ideas, or rants.
2. **Release**: Tap the **RELEASE TO VOID** button.
3. **Vanish**: Watch your thoughts disappear and enjoy the mental space.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff (White)
- **Main Color**: #333 (Dark Gray)
- **Accent**: #eee / #fafafa
- **Radius**: 15px (The Project Standard)

## Privacy Policy
- No login required.
- No tracking or data collection.
- No ads.
- **Zero Retention**: All data is wiped from the browser's memory upon release or refresh.
